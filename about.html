Our Programs

“Love. Care. Compassion”

Help Orphans

A Touch of Kindness.

Acording to UNICEF, an estimated 153 million children worldwide are orphans. The majority of these children don't have a care of a family, they are alone, invisible and unheard. They are being physically, mentally and emotionally damaged.

A Touch of Kindness is advocating for these children around the world and wants you to join the effort.
No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted!

Help Widows

A Touch of Kindness.

What comes from the heart goes to the heart.

According to the United Nations, There are an estimated 258 million widows around the world, and nearly one in ten live in extreme poverty. As women, they have specific needs, but their voices and experiences are often absent from policies that impact their survival.

A Touch of Kindness is advocating for these Widows around the world and wants you to join the effort.
No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted!

Help the Homeless

A Touch of Kindness

Based on national reports, it's estimated that no less than 150 million people, or about 2 percent of the world's population, are homeless.

A Touch of Kindness is advocating for the homeless around the world and wants you to join the effort.
No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted!

Help the needy

A Touch of Kindness

Nearly a half of the world's population (more than 3 billion people) live on less than $2.50 a day. More than 1.3 billion live in extreme poverty — less than $1.25 a day. 1 billion children worldwide are living in poverty. According to UNICEF, 22,000 children die each day due to poverty.

A Touch of Kindness is advocating for people who live in extreme poverty around the world and wants you to join the effort. No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted!
